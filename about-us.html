About Us.

Welcome to the Department of Fiqh & Usul al Fiqh, an integral part of the esteemed Busthanul Uloom Arabic College, located in Paral, Kannur, Kerala, India. Established with a vision to advance the study of Islamic jurisprudence, our department is affiliated with the prestigious Darul Huda Islamic University, Chemmad, a renowned institution dedicated to holistic Islamic education and scholarship.

Our department focuses on the in-depth study of Fiqh and Usul al Fiqh, fostering a nuanced understanding of classical Islamic legal traditions while addressing contemporary issues with scholarly precision. We aim to cultivate a generation of thinkers and practitioners who can seamlessly integrate traditional Islamic knowledge with modern-day challenges, offering solutions rooted in ethical and legal frameworks.

With a dedicated team of experienced scholars and educators, we provide a robust academic program that emphasizes critical analysis, research, and practical application. Our curriculum is designed to empower students with the intellectual tools needed to engage with classical texts, interpret diverse legal methodologies, and contribute meaningfully to the global discourse on Islamic law.

We invite you to join us in our pursuit of knowledge and understanding, contributing to the development of a just and harmonious society through the principles of Fiqh and Usul al Fiqh.
